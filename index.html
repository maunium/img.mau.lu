Quick Edits

It's easy to make simple and quick edits. You can do freeform drawing or draw basic shapes and optionally fill them. You also can write text in the image and draw arrows.

Easy Sharing

mauCapture supports multiple ways of sharing the screenshots. You can upload your image to Imgur or your own mauImageServer. You can also save the image or copy it to the clipboard.
